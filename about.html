About Us

Welcome to SigCent!

We are an easily accesible analysis blog that shares our current stock market perspective and the signals we believe are indicative of future growth trends in Australian and US Stocks.

The 'Signal' and 'Sentiment' ethos
The Signals are both the data outliers; asymmetric value that our stock anaylsis has found AND the near term (<2 year) catalyst that should trigger a market reevaluation that removes the asymmetry.
The Sentiments are our view on what the company's progression will look like up until the catalysing signal and the value oppurtunity available for current investment

We share both the Signals & Sentiments!

To highlight our belief, all shares listed in the sentiment page are part of our current portfolio!
